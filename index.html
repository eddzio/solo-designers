Solo doesn't have to mean alone

Being the only designer at a company can be the most rewarding role of your career.

And it can also be the most challenging.

You are not only responsible for all things design, but also for your own professional growth.

Without managers or peers, it's hard to know whether you're on the right path.

The Solo Designers Collective brings together designers from across the industry to elevate our practice.
From maintaining reliable design systems, to establishing strategic research practices, we talk about what works, and what doesn't.

We also discuss what's expected at different tiers of design seniority.

We share what we know, and learn from each other.

We support each other in the trenches.
